MADHWAI.

Empowering the Streets of India.

We are building the **social, legal, and digital backbone** for 10 million underserved street vendors, formalizing India's ₹8,000 Crore/Day street food economy.

THE PROBLEM: Exclusion, Exploitation, and the Opportunity

No CoV

Vendors lack legal identity (Certificate of Vending), leading to **daily harassment and eviction risk**.

0% Formal

Millions of vendors are excluded from digital payments, loans, insurance, and the **$65B food-tech growth**.

10 Million

**Untapped heroes** serving cheap, authentic food, representing an immense, scalable market opportunity.

Madhwai's Mission: We deliver **justice, dignity, and opportunity** by leveraging the Street Vendors Act (2014) to formalize this massive, fragmented market.

OUR WORK: Formalization, Compliance, and Quick Commerce

1. Legal & Financial Integration

We assist vendors in obtaining the **Certificates of Vending (CoV)** and digital KYC, unlocking financial access (loans, insurance).

2. Hygiene & FSSAI Compliance

Provide mandatory **hygiene training and kits**, guiding vendors to secure FSSAI certification to build consumer trust and meet regulations.

3. Hyperlocal Supply Chain & Delivery

Our local model enables customers to order fresh food for **10–15 minute delivery**, bypassing the inefficiency of centralized dark kitchens.

4. UCO Recycling for Sustainability

We run a circular economy initiative by collecting **Used Cooking Oil (UCO)** for resale to biofuel companies, creating an environmental revenue stream.

THE BENEFITS: Unrivaled Value and Impact

Economic Uplift

  • **Higher Net Income:** Our 20% commission is significantly lower than competitors (25–30%), ensuring vendors keep more profit.
  • **Loan Access:** Formalization grants access to financial tools previously unavailable in the informal sector.
  • **Diversified Revenue:** Vendors earn extra income from selling UCO to Madhwai, supporting sustainability.

Market Disruption

  • **Authentic & Fresh:** We deliver real street food made on the spot, beating the frozen, centralized approach of dark kitchens.
  • **Speed & Cost:** Our local network allows faster, cheaper delivery (10-15 min avg) without high minimum order values.
  • **Variety:** Customers can order from **multiple vendors in a single basket**—a superior experience for street food variety.

Social & Green Returns

  • **Legitimization:** We formalize livelihoods, reducing social stigma and corruption (bribes/harassment).
  • **Government Data:** We provide valuable data from a previously opaque sector, enabling better policy and taxation.
  • **Environmental:** The UCO recycling chain promotes a clean, circular economy aligning with ESG goals.

FINANCIAL BLUEPRINT & MULTI-STREAM REVENUE

DIVERSIFIED REVENUE STREAMS

1. Order Commission (20%): Core revenue stream from every successful delivery (below competitors' 25-30%).

2. Vendor Support Fees: Charges for legal assistance (CoV registration) and hygiene compliance kits/training.

3. Customer Convenience Fee: Small, consistent fee on customer orders.

4. UCO Recycling Margin: Revenue generated by aggregating and reselling Used Cooking Oil to biodiesel companies.
